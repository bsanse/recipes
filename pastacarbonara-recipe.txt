Ingredients:
- 500 g pasta
- 100 g bacon
- 4 eggs
- Pepper
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt
- Water

Directions:
1. Boil some water and add the pasta until it is aldente.

2. Fry the bacon with some pepper in a pan.

3. Grate de cheese and beat it with the four eggs and some salt.

4. Mix the souce with the pasta.
